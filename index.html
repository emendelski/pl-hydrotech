Problem

Planet Earth is drying up. Climate change and the risk of draught have come to the forefront of threats to our globe. In 2019, a whopping 81 countries were classified as being under medium or high threat from water shortage. And what does such water shortage actually mean for Earth’s population? Poor sanitation in many developing countries, and increasingly, a difficulty in managing agriculture. Without adequate water supply, fruits, vegetables and crops won’t grow.

The beginning of 2020 brought bush fires across Australia – made so much worse by a very dry winter the previous year, which gave perfect conditions for small fires to spread rapidly, causing havoc in their wake. Approximately 66% of Africa is arid and 300 million people live in areas suffering from water scarcity. The Middle East, though rich in oil, also suffers from water shortage. Even in parts of the world where water is not scarce, managing the supply and usage is increasingly troublesome.

Solution

Over the past 20 years through state of the art scientific research and innovative design, we have created a solution for dealing with water management across the globe. We have done so by using the most advanced environment friendly solution - Nano Technology. A new science, engineering and technology conducted at the nanoscale helps to solve one of the biggest world problems - the global water crisis. NanoGel helps to sustain water in the ground – aligned with the natural growth cycle of plants. NanoGel slowly and gradually absorbs water (500ml from 1gr of NanoGel) which is the highest absorption factor available globally. This method allows for full cooperation with the probiotics, bacteria and other microorganism living in root systems. Using NanoGel is ecologically friendly as it is 100% biodegradable, water is retained and supplied to plants evenly, and the use of hydrogels can reduce the need for using pesticides and fertilisers. NanoGel is patented for use in agriculture - the only such “hydrogel” with such a patent on the market.

Our unique patented solution additional enables the injection of NanoGel into the ground.

Technology

Our company is the owner of the world patent for the unique method of application of NanoGel into the soil. Special equipment designed at our R&D centre can inject hydrogel straight into the ground, without the need to unsettle crops. This means it can be used at any point of the plant development cycle. The HydroTech machine is compact – and so easy to transport. It’s cutting edge innovation comes from the combination of NanoGel and the way its implemented into the soil. NanoGel is safe for the environment. It is neutral to soil microorganisms, non-toxic for humans, animals and plants. NanoGel is the only product in the world that has a patent for usage with food production.

About us

HydroTech was established in the UK to expand the global distribution of NanoGel together with the specialised injection machine. We cooperate with the local and international R&D centres, public and private authorities. NanoGel was developed at the Poland based company Art Agro - an R&D company that has been developing this technology for the past 25 years and currently is the only global producer of NanoGels that has a patent for usage with food production.

Art Agro also has a global distribution network and a certification already in place, as follows: India, UAE, RPA, Russia, Croatia, Canada, Colambia, Rwanda, Itally, Germany, Poland
